Course Title:Software Process Improvement

Catalogue Description:

This course covers the following topics: Introduction to Software Processes Improvement; Prescriptive Process Models; Descriptive Process Models; Software Process Improvement Modeling; Continuous Improvement Approaches; CMM & CMMI; Software Process Improvement Tool; and Software Process Simulation.

Unit wise Major Topics:

Unit Topic No. of teaching hours
Total Contact Hours 45
1 Software Processes Improvement: Introduction, Software Process Modeling & Improvement, and Process Modeling Goals and Benefits. 6
2 CMM & CMMI: Levels, Key Process Areas; and Continuous Improvement Approaches. 15
3 Prescriptive Process Models: Prescriptive Process Model Classes, Process Standards, Process Representations in Organization, and Deploying Prescriptive Process Model. 12
4 Descriptive Process Models: Goals, Developing a Descriptive Process Model, Descriptive Process Modeling Alternative, Guidelines for Process Elicitation Interview, and Managing Risk in Descriptive Process Modeling Efforts. 4.5
5 Software Process Improvement Modeling: Criteria for Assessing Process Modeling Notation, Multi-view Process Modeling Language, Software Process Engineering Metamodel; and Tools Support. 3
6 Software Process Simulation: Method, Models, and Combining Process Simulation & Empirical Studies. 4.5
